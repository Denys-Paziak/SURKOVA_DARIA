Can youhelp redesign our logo or create a new brand identity?

Can you help redesign our logo or create a new brand identity? Whether it's an established brand, or a new business build from scratch, my team will ensure to create a tailored brand identity, with all the touchpoints included ( logotype, colour, brand strategy, typography, assets etc.)

How much will this project cost, and what’s included in the pricing?

How much will this project cost, and what’s included in the pricing? We don't charge a default price for every client, since the project deliverables, timeline, and scope of work can vary from business to business. Please don't be shy to reach out to us and discuss the project investment, with no obligations attached.

How long will the design project take?

How long will the design project take? Just as the price, the timeline is a tricky and versatile thing to estimate. Usually, simple graphic design request , such as posters or stickers will take up to a week to create, while brand identities can take 2-8 weeks to complete. We're always open to chat personally to discuss a suitable timeframe for your brand.

How will the design process work, and how many revisions are included?

How will the design process work, and how many revisions are included? We use the one-concept method, where we thoroughly analyse the background of your brand( competitors, target audience etc.) and then come up with the most suitable offer. We use notion to keep track of the project as well as client communication. There are always 3 revisions included in the price, all the other ones are to be charged. But.. it almost never comes to that )

I have a concept in my head, but don't know how to implement it..

I have a concept in my head, but don't know how to implement it.. Not a problem at all! We will gladly discuss any thoughts you have and help you understand what exactly can be useful for your business. Of course, it's on the house, no charge or obligations

What file formats will I receive, and will I own the rights to the designs?

What file formats will I receive, and will I own the rights to the designs? All the rights belong solely to your and your brand after the completion of the project. My team will provide you with all the necessary files with your assets , like Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign and so on. You will also receive a small guideline of how to use them all.
